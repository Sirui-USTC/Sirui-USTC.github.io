Biography

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in June 2023, under the supervision of Professor Chen Enhong. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at USTC. I am a member of the China Computer Federation, a committee member of the Affective Computing Specialist Committee and the Youth Working Committee of the China National Committee for Chinese Information Processing, and the Secretary-General of the Affective Computing Specialist Committee of the Anhui Province Artificial Intelligence Society. My research focuses on affective computing, multimodal understanding, and human-computer interaction. In recent years, I have published more than 30 papers in important journals and conferences in the fields of affective computing, multimodal understanding, and deep learning, such as IEEE Transactions on Affective Computing, Neural Networks, and ACM Transactions on Multimedia Computing, Communications, and Applications, including 16 first-authored or corresponding papers. I have applied for more than 10 Chinese invention patents, with 7 authorized. I have received 7 awards for academic papers and competitions both domestically and internationally. I am leading one National Natural Science Foundation of China Youth Fund project and one Sichuan Province Natural Science Foundation Youth Fund project, and I have participated in several other major research projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China and the Ministry of Science and Technology of China. I serve as a Guest Editor for the academic journal Frontiers in Big Data and as the Chair of the Special Sessions at the international conference PRAI2022/2023/2024. I am also a reviewer for several academic journals, including IEEE Transactions on Affective Computing and Neural Networks.
