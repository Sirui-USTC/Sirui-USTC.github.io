Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the School of Computer Science and Technology,   University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), and a member of the   State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Intelligence. I received my Ph.D. in the School of Computer Science, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in June 2023, supervised by Prof.  Enhong Chen. Before starting my Ph.D. study, I worked at MediaTek Inc. from 2017 to 2019. Earlier, I received my Master's degree in 2014 and my Bachelor's degree in 2017, both from Southwest University of Science and Technology. I am broadly interested in affective computing, multimodal understanding, and human-computer interactionstrong>.
